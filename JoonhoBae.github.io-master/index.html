About

“Judge your success by the degree that you’re enjoying peace, health, and love”
- H. Jackson Brown Jr.

My research is concerned with the development of data-driven approaches to identify, monitor, and control complex dynamical systems. Motivated by a multidisciplinary background from statistics, financial economics, and industrial engineering, I made on the topic of operations management and management science based on data-driven, probabilistic models combined with machine learning techniques.

Previously, I worked as a full-time marketing manager at SK Telecom and co-founded and led a start-up for blind masseurs (Cham Son Gil Cooperative). I received my M.S. in Industrial & Systems Engineering from KAIST, and B.S. in Statistics and Financial Economics from Seoul National University.
